IMPORTANT 1. Set Your WhatsApp Number

This is the first thing you must do. Your WhatsApp number appears in many places.

1 Open this file in VS Code:

assets/js/main.js

2 Find this line at the very top (line 6):

const WHATSAPP_NUMBER = '91XXXXXXXXXX';

3 Replace 91XXXXXXXXXX with your real number:

const WHATSAPP_NUMBER = '919876543210';
Format: Start with 91 (India code) + your 10-digit number. No spaces, no dashes, no plus sign.
Example: If your number is 98765 43210, type: 919876543210

4 Now open this file:

index.html

Press Cmd + Shift + H (this opens Find & Replace for all files)

In the top box, type: 91XXXXXXXXXX

In the bottom box, type: your real number (e.g. 919876543210)

Click the button that says "Replace All" (the icon with two arrows)

Done! Every WhatsApp button on your website now goes to your real number.

2. Change Product Prices

1 Open this file:

assets/js/products.js

2 Find the product you want to change. Look for its name. Then find the price line:

price: "₹XXX",

3 Change it to your real price:

price: "₹799",

You can also show a range:

price: "₹599 – ₹899",
Don't delete the quotes " " or the comma , — they need to stay exactly as they are. Only change the words inside the quotes.

3. Add or Remove Products

1 Open this file:

assets/js/products.js

To add a new product:

2 Copy this entire block and paste it just before the last ]; at the bottom of the file:

{ name: "Your Product Name", description: "A short description of the product", price: "₹799", sizes: "M, L, XL, XXL", photo: "", badge: "", fabric: "Pure Cotton", occasion: "Daily Wear", stock: "", whatsapp: "919876543210" },

3 Change the highlighted parts to your product details.

To remove a product:

Find the product block (from { to },) and delete the whole thing.

Quick reference for special fields:

FieldOptions
badge"Bestseller" or "New Arrival" or "" (empty = no badge)
occasion"Daily Wear", "Office Wear", "Festive", "Outings"
stock"Selling Fast", "Only 3 Left", "Limited Stock", or "" (empty = don't show)

4. Add Product Photos

1 Prepare your photo:

  • Use a vertical/portrait photo (taller than wide)
  • Recommended size: 800 x 1000 pixels
  • Save as .jpg
  • Name it in lowercase with dashes: cotton-kurtha-teal.jpg

2 Put the photo in this folder:

assets/images/products/

Just drag the photo into this folder in VS Code or Finder.

3 Tell the website about it:

Open assets/js/products.js

Find your product and change the photo line from empty to:

photo: "assets/images/products/cotton-kurtha-teal.jpg",
Photo naming rules: All lowercase. No spaces. Use dashes between words.
Good: floral-kurta-pink.jpg
Bad: Floral Kurta Pink.jpg

5. Add Your Brand Photo (About Section)

1 Save your brand photo as about.jpg

2 Put it in: assets/images/brand/

3 Open index.html

Press Cmd + F and search for: Your Brand Photo

You'll find this line:

<div class="img-placeholder"><span>Your Brand Photo</span></div>

4 Replace that entire line with:

<img src="assets/images/brand/about.jpg" alt="YourBrand" />

6. Add Instagram Screenshots

1 Take 4 screenshots of your best Instagram posts

2 Save them as: post1.jpg, post2.jpg, post3.jpg, post4.jpg

3 Put them in: assets/images/instagram/

4 Open index.html

Press Cmd + F and search for: insta-post-placeholder

You'll find 4 lines like this:

<div class="insta-post-placeholder"></div>

5 Replace each one with (change the number for each):

<img src="assets/images/instagram/post1.jpg" alt="Instagram post" style="width:100%;height:100%;object-fit:cover;border-radius:4px;" />

7. Change Any Text on the Website

1 Open index.html

2 Press Cmd + F

3 Type the text you want to change (e.g. "Elegance, Woven Into")

4 Change the words. Don't touch anything inside < > brackets.

Example

You see: <h1>Elegance, Woven Into<br /><em>Every Detail</em></h1>

You can change: Elegance, Woven Into and Every Detail

Don't touch: <h1>, <br />, <em>, </em>, </h1>

Golden rule: Only change the words you can read as normal text. Never delete or change anything inside the < > angle brackets.

LIVE How to Go Live

We use Netlify — it's free and the easiest option for your website.

First time (putting your site online):

1 Go to netlify.com in your browser

2 Click Sign Up — use your Google account or email

3 After signing in, you'll see a big area that says "Drag and drop your site folder here"

4 Open Finder on your Mac

5 Find your web designing folder

6 Drag the entire folder onto that area in Netlify

7 Wait 10-30 seconds

Your site is now live! Netlify gives you a free link like:
happy-panda-12345.netlify.app
You can share this link immediately.

Getting your own domain (like yourstore.com):

1 Buy a domain from GoDaddy, Namecheap, or Hostinger (~₹500-800/year)

2 In Netlify, go to Site Settings → Domain Management → Add Custom Domain

3 Type your domain (e.g. yourstore.com)

4 Netlify will show you DNS settings — contact your domain provider's support chat and ask them: "Please point my domain to Netlify. Here are the nameservers." Share the nameservers Netlify shows you.

Note: Domain connection can take 1-24 hours to start working. This is normal.

UPDATE How to Update Your Live Site

Every time you make changes and want them to show on your live website:

1 Make your changes in VS Code (change text, add photos, update prices — anything)

2 Save the files (Cmd + S)

3 Go to netlify.com → click on your site

4 Click Deploys tab at the top

5 You'll see the same drag-and-drop area

6 Drag your entire folder again

7 Wait 10-30 seconds — done! Your site is updated.

That's it. The full process every time:

Edit in VS Code → Save → Drag folder to Netlify → Live in 30 seconds

Common Questions

Q: Do I need to pay for Netlify?

No. Netlify is free for personal/small business sites. You only pay for your domain name.

Q: Can I preview changes before going live?

Yes! Just double-click your index.html file — it opens in your browser. Check everything looks good, then drag to Netlify.

Q: I made a mistake and my site looks broken. What do I do?

In VS Code, press Cmd + Z multiple times to undo your changes. Save the file, then drag your folder to Netlify again.

Q: How long does it take for changes to show?

About 30 seconds after you drag the folder to Netlify. Sometimes you need to clear your browser cache — press Cmd + Shift + R to hard refresh.

Q: Can I edit from my phone?

No. Always edit from your Mac using VS Code.
